FIND THE CHICKS! DIG FOR SNACKS! ANNOY THE DUCKS! NAVIGATE THE MAZE!

MAZE DOG is a cute little adventure game for 1 player, sporting a seriously rad theme tune and a simple yet fun aesthetic! You go from screen to screen controlling a cute little puppy. You need to find 8 chickens that has run away from their home and theres’ 8 “snacks” hidden throughout the game for completionists. Going from screen to screen can be a little dull sometimes, but finding a chick does feel rewarding. Plus there are a bunch of little details that I enjoy, like pushing bulldogs into puddles or going to the beach to bark at ducks (don’t do it!).

Try MAZE DOG with a steamy cup of java this afternoon.
