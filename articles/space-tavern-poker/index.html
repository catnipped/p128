I suck at gambling. But I still find poker to be a fun pastime. In Space Tavern Poker you’re stuck on a spaceport, low on space cash. You can take a seat at one of three tables (low, mid, high stakes) to play a hand or two of space poker. The AI-players have a tiny bit of personality, with different names and playing styles. Each player also has a spaceship that they will bet when they’re low on cash. Try to collect them all! Or, like me, sell your own ship so you can play a little more before you go bankrupt. If you want a light-hearted poker experience, you’ll like Space Tavern Poker.
