Did you play DALEKS, the old Mac game from the early nineties? It’s ok if you didn’t. In DALEK, a faithful remake, you play as a lone stranger stuck on a huge grid with a bunch of Daleks (the weird villain race from Dr.Who). You can move your stranger in 8 directions and each time you move, the Daleks will move towards you. However, if two Daleks move to a space, they collide and crush each other. The goal of the game is to move your stranger so that all daleks die! EXTERMINATE THE EXTERMINATORS!

The game is quite tricky but charming. I just wish there were more variety as all levels play exactly the same.
