P128 — tiny reviews of tiny games — is a blog with reviews of PICO-8 games. PICO-8 is an 8-bit “Fantasy Console”, sort of like an emulator, but with developer tools built in.

All reviews on this site are written in 128 words or less.

P128 is made and edited by me, Ossian ‘catnipped’ Borén, and indie artist and game developer. If you want to support my work, please share it and/or give me some money on Patreon.
