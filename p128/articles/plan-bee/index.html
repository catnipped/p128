In Plan Bee you play as a “bee”. A bee is a type of flying insect that plays a super important part of the eco-system. It “pollinates” the flowers by taking “pollen” from one flower to another, helping the flowers create new flowers, ensuring eco-diversity. It is your job as a player to navigate from flower to flower, collecting the pollen. But your bee is dizzy and starving, probably from colony collapse disorder, and will die soon should you fail to find new pollen in time or collapse to the ground!

Plan Bee is a fun little 5 minute high-score single player game, sorta like Flappy Bird.
